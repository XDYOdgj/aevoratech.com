Keystone 3-in-1 Portable Air Conditioner

Designed for small spaces, this versatile unit combines cooling, dehumidifying, and air circulation in one convenient device. With 8500 BTU cooling power, it keeps your room comfortable during the hot summer months. The built-in dehumidifier removes up to 2 pints of moisture per hour, ensuring a drier and healthier indoor environment.

Efficient Cooling & Dehumidification

This unit is perfect for apartments, bedrooms, or small rooms up to 200 square feet. Whether you're dealing with high humidity or just want to stay cool, the 8500 BTU power and dehumidifier work together to create a balanced climate.

User-Friendly Controls

Enjoy easy operation with the included remote control and 3 fan speed settings. You can customize your cooling experience to match your comfort level and daily routine. Plus, the cleanable air filter ensures optimal airflow and reminds you when it's time for maintenance.

Portable & Reliable

Featuring 4-way caster wheels and side handles, this unit is easy to move from room to room. The auto restart feature keeps your comfort uninterrupted even during power outages, so you never have to worry about losing your cooling settings.

This portable air conditioner with dehumidifier is a great solution for those looking to maintain a cooler and healthier indoor atmosphere without the hassle of installation. It's an ideal cooling unit for small rooms and a great addition to your home or office.
