Acekool 12000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner

Experience powerful cooling for large rooms up to 450 square feet with the Acekool 12000 BTU portable air conditioner. This compact yet highly effective unit is designed with convenience in mind, offering three-in-one functionality as an air conditioner, dehumidifier, and fan. Whether you're looking to manage humidity, circulate fresh air, or simply cool down your space, this unit delivers it all without the need for permanent installation.

Equipped with a self-evaporation system, it eliminates the need for frequent draining, making maintenance easier and more efficient. The built-in remote control allows for easy temperature adjustments, while the included window kit simplifies installation and ensures proper ventilation. With features like a 24-hour timer and sleep mode, you can customize your cooling experience to save energy and money, even during the night.

Thanks to its lightweight and portable design, this unit is easy to move from room to room. Ideal for apartments, offices, or spaces without central air, the Acekool 12000 BTU portable air conditioner is a smart choice for anyone who values comfort, efficiency, and convenience in their home environment.
