Acekool Portable Air Conditioner

Stay comfortably cool all year round with the Acekool portable air conditioner. Combining powerful cooling, dehumidification, and ventilation in one sleek unit, it’s perfect for homes, offices, or even your RV.

Powerful Cooling for Larger Spaces

The Acekool portable air conditioner is equipped with a 14,000 BTU cooling system, making it perfect for cooling large rooms up to 750 square feet. Whether it’s a sweltering summer day or a humid evening, it delivers strong and efficient cooling to keep you refreshed.

3-in-1 Functionality for Versatility

Designed with multifunktiness in mind, this unit features cooling, dehumidification, and fan modes in one. It’s not just a cooler — it’s a complete climate control solution for your home or business. Switch between modes effortlessly to suit your needs.

Smart Features for Ease of Use

Enhance your comfort with advanced auto and sleep modes that adjust cooling automatically and minimize noise whenever bedroom hours arrive. Plus, integrate it with a digital LCD control panel and remote control for seamless operation and precise settings even from across the room.

Quiet & Eco-Friendly Design

Enjoy quiet operation with a built-in exhaust system that eliminates the need for a separate vent. It’s effortless to install and maintain — just a window and an outlet are required. Additionally, the use of eco-friendly R-32 refrigerant ensures a greener environment and improves operational efficiencies greatly, up to thirty percent.

Convenience You Can Rely On

With a 24-hour programmable timer, you can automate your cooling schedule and enjoy maximum convenience. This unit is the perfect companion for anyone looking to maintain a cooler atmosphere, reduce humidity effectively, or simply enjoy a more comfortable living environment, all year long.

Perfect for Any Space

Whether you're looking to cool large rooms, bedrooms, living areas, or even an office or RV, the Acekool unit fits right in. It's easy to bring wherever you need it—instant cooling wherever convenience is needed.
