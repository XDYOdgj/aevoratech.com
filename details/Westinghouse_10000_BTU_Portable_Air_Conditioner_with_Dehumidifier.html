Efficient and Versatile Cooling for Any Space

This Westinghouse portable air conditioner is designed to keep your small spaces cool, comfortable, and fresh. With a powerful 10,000 BTU cooling capacity, it can effectively cool rooms up to 450 square feet, making it perfect for use in bedrooms, offices, or family rooms. Whether you're dealing with sweltering heat or high humidity, its multi-functional operation allows you to switch between cooling, fan, and dehumidifying modes to suit your needs.

Enjoy quiet operation at just 51 dBA, ideal for uninterrupted sleep and daily use. Features like sleep mode and a programmable timer ensure energy efficiency and convenience. The unit comes with everything you need for a quick setup, including an exhaust duct and a window vent kit, so you can install it in under five minutes without making permanent changes to your space.

The included remote control lets you adjust temperature and settings from up to 20 feet away, making it easy to use while relaxing. With its compact size and user-friendly interface, this portable air conditioner is a great addition to your home, office, or recreational vehicle. Keep your environment fresh, cool, and healthy with this all-in-one cooling solution.
