Della Motto Series Mini Split AC

Achieve ideal comfort all year round with the Della Motto Series Mini Split AC. Featuring a high 17 SEER2 rating and a cool 9000 BTU capacity, this unit delivers exceptional energy efficiency and powerful performance. It offers both cooling and heating, making it a versatile solution for any space.

Designed with a sleek, compact form, it easily integrates into your home without the need for ductwork. The pre-charged 3/4 ton heat pump ensures optimal operation and consistent temperature control, while its smart Alexa and WiFi compatibility lets you manage your climate from your phone or voice assistant.

Key Features

  • Energy-efficient cooling with 17 SEER2 rating
  • Smart sensor technology for personalized temperature control
  • Cooling and heating up to 450 square feet
  • Quiet operation at only 43 dBa
  • Easy-to-use smart functions with 4D air flow

Why Choose Della Motto Series Mini Split AC?

Whether you need to cool a living room, bedroom, or office, this ductless split system is the perfect choice. Its advanced smart sensor detects temperature at the remote control, not the indoor unit, for more precise and efficient climate control. The use of environmentally friendly R32 refrigerant minimizes your carbon footprint and ensures faster, more efficient cooling.

Enjoy a peaceful, comfortable environment with its ultra-quiet operation, and customize your settings with multiple fan speeds and modes. This mini split air conditioner combines technology and sustainability to provide your home with modern, reliable comfort solutions.
