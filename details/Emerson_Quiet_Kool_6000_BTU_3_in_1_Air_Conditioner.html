Emerson Quiet Kool 6000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner

Boasting a sleek 3-in-1 design, this portable air conditioner combines cooling, dehumidification, and fan functions into one compact unit. Perfect for spaces up to 350 sq. ft., it ensures optimal climate control with minimal effort, eliminating the need for frequent draining thanks to its bucketless system. The unit operates quietly at just 50dB, making it an ideal choice for bedrooms and office environments where noise is a concern.

Smart Features for Modern Living

With smart home integration, you can easily control Emerson Quiet Kool using your voice via Alexa or Google Home, or conveniently manage it from your smartphone. This level of connectivity adds convenience to your daily routine while keeping your space cool and dry. The programmable timer and sleep mode let you customize cooling schedules to match your lifestyle, ensuring energy efficiency and comfort at the right times.

Efficient Dehumidification at Its Core

Emerson Quiet Kool is equipped with a powerful dehumidifier that removes up to 4.2 pints of moisture per hour, helping to maintain a comfortable humidity level and improve indoor air quality. Whether you're cooling a living room, dehumidifying a basement, or simply enhancing airflow, this unit is built to handle a variety of needs with ease and effectiveness.

Why Choose Emerson Quiet Kool?

Ideal for small to medium rooms, this unit brings convenience, comfort, and efficiency to your home. Its quiet operation and smart design make it an excellent choice for everyday use, upholding a clean and enjoyable living environment without compromise.
